Vegan Ratatouille

What is Ratatouille?

Ratatouille is a classic french vegetable stew or mediterranean oven roasted vegetable dish. It is loaded with fresh vegetables such as zucchini, tomatoes and eggplant. Often yellow squash as well as red bell peppers are added, but you can get creative depending on waht you have in your fridge

4 servings

20 min prep time

50 min cook time

Total 1 hr 10min

Ingredients

  • 1-2 tbsp olive oil + more for drizzling
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves minced
  • 17.6 oz strained tomatoes or passata
  • 1 tbsp dried Italian herbs e.g. basil, oregano, thyme
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 eggplant
  • 3 tomatoes
  • Vegan mozzarella optional
  • Vegan cheese grated

For garnish (optional)

  • Vegan parmesan
  • Basil

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 392
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet and saute the onions for 2-3 minutes until translucent. Then add the garlic and saute for anohter minute
  3. Now add the tomato paste and the strained tomatoes. Season with salt, pepper and herbs and simmer, covered for 5 minutes
  4. Meanwhile, slice the zucchini, eggplant and tomatoes into 0.1-inch (2-3mm) thin slices
  5. Pour the tomato sauce into a round 9.5 inche (24cm) baking dish (or leave it in the skillet/pan if it is ovenproof) and arrange the vegetables alternatley on top. Season with salt, pepper and herbs. Arrange vegan mini mozzerall balls and grated vegan cheese ontop and drizzle a little olive oil over the top.
  6. Cover the pan with foil or a lid and bake in the preheated oven for about 30 minutes. Then uncover and bake for another 15 minutes, until the vegetables are cooked and lightly brown
  7. Garnish your Ratatouille with vegan parmesan, thyme and basil

Reciepe Notes

  1. Vegetables- if you like, you can also saute diced carrots, bell peppers or celery and add them to the tomatoe sauce
  2. Meal prep- The tomato sauce can be prepared in advance. Then simply pour into a sealed jar and store in the refrigerator until ready to use
  3. Storing- The remaining Ratatouille can be stored covered in the refrigerator for up to 1 week. Then simply reheat in the oven for 15 minutes or shorter in the microwave
